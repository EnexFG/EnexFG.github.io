This dataset accompanies research on fiscal policy, oil-revenue shocks, and the external balance in Ecuador.

It is used in [García-Albán et al. 2021](https://enexfg.github.io/research/2021-08-01-paper-title-number-2) and García-Albán and Camacho (2025).

[Download CSV](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/EnexFG/EnexFG.github.io/refs/heads/master/_datasets_files/fds1.csv)
