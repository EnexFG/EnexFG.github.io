Freddy García-Albán
===================

Applied econometrician building decision systems for policy, business, and research.

I work at the intersection of macroeconometrics, forecasting, fiscal analysis, and high-frequency measurement. I currently serve as Chief Economist at Andersen Ecuador and teach at ESPOL and Ecotec.

My work combines technical rigor with institutional judgment: I build models, produce applied research, and translate evidence into decisions for academic, public-policy, and private-sector audiences.

What I Do
---------

* Macroeconomic forecasting and nowcasting
* Fiscal and policy analysis
* Bayesian econometrics and time-series modeling
* High-frequency measurement and data systems
* Applied NLP and quantitative research

Current Focus
-------------

* Real-time tracking of economic activity in Ecuador
* Fiscal policy, oil shocks, and macroeconomic adjustment
* External balance and twin-deficits dynamics in a dollarized economy
* Competition policy, simulation, and market analysis
